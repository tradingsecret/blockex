Imperium Protocol is a cryptocurrency blockchain based on the MimbleWimble protocol. MimbleWimble is an innovative technology that brings scalability to private blockchain transactions.

Our blockchain offers two types of transactions: regular transactions with fast speed and slower transactions but are fully anonymous and untraceable.

Arctis is a testnet coin that has no value and was created only for public tests.
